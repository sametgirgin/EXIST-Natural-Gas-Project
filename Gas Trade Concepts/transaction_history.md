SGP Transaction History for the Continuous Trading Platform (SGP) in the Turkish natural gas market lists the individual trades (matches) between buyers and sellers on the spot market.

#### Column Breakdown 

- Date & Hour: These represent the matching time, indicating exactly when the trade was executed between two participants.
- Contract: This identifies the specific gas delivery day using the format GGYYYYMMDD. For example, a contract named GG20260211 is for gas to be delivered on February 11, 2026.
    Expert Note: You will notice "intra-day" and "day-ahead" activity here. Some trades for a specific contract date happen on that actual day, while others happen a day or two prior
- Price: The value in TL/1000 $Sm^3$. This is the price at which the transaction was cleared. In your screenshot, prices are hovering around 13,800 to 14,500 TL.
- Matching Quantity: The volume of gas traded, expressed in 1000 $Sm^3$. For instance, a quantity of "255" represents 255,000 standard cubic meters of natural gas.

#### Trading Insight
Looking at the data, you can see multiple transactions for the same contract (e.g., GG20260211) at different prices. This reflects the volatility and liquidity of the market as traders adjust their positions to balance their portfolios before the delivery window closes.
